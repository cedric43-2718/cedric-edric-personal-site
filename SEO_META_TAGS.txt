To use these meta tags:

1. Replace "https://yoursite.com" with your actual domain
2. Replace "@yourhandle" with your Twitter handle
3. Create og-image.jpg (1200x630px recommended) and twitter-image.jpg (1200x675px) for social sharing
4. Create a profile image for the JSON-LD structured data
5. Add an apple-touch-icon.png (180x180px) for Apple devices
6. Update the theme-color to match your site's primary color
7. All meta tags should be placed in the <head> section of your index.html

Additional recommendations:
- Ensure your site has fast load times (Core Web Vitals optimization)
- Add a sitemap.xml and robots.txt to your public folder
- Submit your site to Google Search Console and Bing Webmaster Tools
- Create descriptive alt text for all images
- Use header tags (H1, H2, H3) properly throughout your pages
- Ensure mobile responsiveness (you're already using viewport meta tag)
